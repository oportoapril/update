"It is not the length of life, but depth of life."

My personal philosophy when it comes to education and learning is closely related to several cognitive theories of motivation.
I believe that what goes on the classroom is everyone's responsibility and by interacting with other students,
all of us can create an ideal environment for education, safety, understanding and even fun.
